## How to use this dataset across the JobStrike build

### Immediate (Day 2–6 of build)
1. **Tier 2 ATS feasibility study** — for the **Top 50 hot-growth list**, visit each company's careers page, identify the ATS they use (Greenhouse, Lever, SmartRecruiters, Ashby, Workday), record the slug. Target: at least 20 of 50 reachable via public ATS endpoints.
2. Add to a new `employer_targets` table in Supabase on day 1 of build, with columns: `apollo_id, name, domain, band, ats_type, ats_slug, ats_verified, growth_12m, priority_tier`.

### Month 4–5 (verification layer)
3. Re-validate these companies with Apollo one-time batch enrichment (or Clearbit if Apollo subscription has lapsed) to confirm they're still UAE-active.
4. Use the `id` field to pull active job postings per company (Task 2 below) and populate JobStrike's own listings feed.

### Month 6 (concierge employer phase)
5. **From the Top 50 hot-growth list, pick 10 companies** whose sectors match your candidate base. Approach each with the personal pitch I drafted earlier: "I have 400 mid-career candidates in your sector, would you review 5 hand-picked shortlist entries for one of your open roles?"
6. The remaining 40 are your next-wave pipeline.

### Month 9+ (self-serve employer portal)
7. The full 989 list becomes your employer-portal invite pool. Segment by band, growth, sector — invite in cohorts, not all at once.

## Critical caveats

1. **Size bands are loose.** See Emaar example above. Use revenue + growth + founded-year as sanity checks, not the band alone.
2. **Phone verification is a proxy, not proof.** 385 companies have +971 numbers in Apollo, 340 have non-UAE phones (often corporate HQ in another country with a UAE office). Don't exclude non-+971 companies automatically — cross-check their `organization_job_locations` to confirm they actually hire in UAE.
3. **"Currently hiring" is Apollo's flag, not yours.** Re-validate before approaching any company — Apollo's hiring signal can be 1–6 weeks stale.
4. **No email/phone reveal was performed.** To contact a specific decision-maker at any of these companies, you'll need a separate People Enrichment call (credit-burning). Save that for the 10 companies you actually want to approach in Month 6.
5. **NAICS/SIC industry codes are included but not decoded** in this summary. If you need industry grouping, decode via the NAICS 2022 lookup table (public).
