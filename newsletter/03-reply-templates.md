brief: never manage the reader, answer them. Each template concedes
honestly, then points to one next step and one free tool. Short and warm.
Edit the bracketed parts per conversation. British English, no em dashes.

These are drafts only. Send each reply as a normal one-to-one email reply
from info@inspireambitions.com, not a bulk send.
